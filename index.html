About Me
Researcher at Naver Labs Europe and MIAI Grenoble
Principal Research Scientist and Team Lead at Naver Labs Europe.
Research on continual, incremental, online and self-supervised learning,
with applications to the understanding of the semantic and geometry of complex scenes.
Recent projects
EPIC Fields @NeurIPS23

Extension of EPIC KITCHENS dataset with 3D information. Collaboration with VGG, Oxford and MaVi, Bristol. NeurIPS 2023

ImageNet-SD @CVPR23

Pretraining only using synthetic images obtained with Stable Diffusion. Collaboration with Thoth, Inria. CVPR 2023

SLACK @CVPR23

Automatic data-augmentation for classification. Collaboration with Thoth, Inria. ICLR 2023

t-Rex @ICLR23

Supervised pretraining for strong results both on ImageNet and on transfer. Collaboration with Thoth, Inria. ICLR 2023

N3F @3DV22

Fusing SSL (eg DINO) 2D representations with NeRF-like models using distillation. Collaboration with VGG, Oxford. 3DV 2022

CoG @ICCV21

A concept generalization benchmark for visual representations, named ImageNet-CoG. Collaboration with Thoth, Inria. ICCV 2021
