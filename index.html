Recent projects
UNIC @ECCV24

A UNIC classification model that distills from strong pretrained models, and performs on par of better then each of them. ECCV 2024

PANDAS @CoLLAs24

Automatically discover novel classes from unlabeled images and add the discovered classes to extend an existing detector. CoLLAs 2024

Ret4Loc @ICLR24

Weatherproofing Retrieval for Localization: universal retrieval model enhanced with synthetic images during training. ICLR 2024

EPIC Fields @NeurIPS23

Extension of EPIC KITCHENS dataset with 3D information. Collaboration with VGG, Oxford and MaVi, Bristol. NeurIPS 2023

A fully synthetic ImageNet @CVPR23

Pretraining only using synthetic images obtained with Stable Diffusion. Collaboration with Thoth, Inria. CVPR 2023

t-Rex @ICLR23

Supervised pretraining for strong results both on ImageNet and on transfer. Collaboration with Thoth, Inria. ICLR 2023
